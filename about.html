Our Mission

About CADRIA

CADRIA — Consensus-Anchored Distributed Review of Intelligent Agents — exists to make the deployment of increasingly capable AI systems safe, trustworthy, and accountable.

The Problem

Stronger AI demands stronger oversight

As AI systems begin to exceed the capability of any individual reviewer, traditional oversight breaks down. A single human cannot reliably check the work of a system that operates faster, broader, and deeper than they can. CADRIA's answer: a network of many weaker, specialised Human Digital Representatives that collectively oversee stronger AI — anchored in consensus, bounded by worst-case risk, and ultimately governed by humans.

Key Principles

What CADRIA is built on

♻️

Scalable Oversight

Many weaker HDRs collectively oversee stronger AI. Oversight capacity grows with the network, not with any single reviewer's ability.

🛡️

Deployment-Time Control

Real-time gating, bounding, and escalation of actions — control is applied at the moment it matters, before outputs reach the world.

⚠️

Robust to Strategic Pressure

Randomised HDR selection, diverse evaluation criteria, and full auditability make the system resistant to gaming, collusion, and manipulation — including by the AI under review.

👤

Human-in-the-Loop

Humans retain ultimate authority for high-impact decisions. CADRIA augments human judgment; it never replaces it.
